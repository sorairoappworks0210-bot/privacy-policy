1. Data We Collect

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. For ad delivery and measurement, Device ID, Advertising Data, and Product Interaction data may be collected.

2. Tracking

This app follows Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT). Permission is requested when required. Core features remain available even if tracking is denied.

3. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are provided via Apple StoreKit. Payment information is processed by Apple, and we do not access credit card details.

4. On-Device Data

Your artwork, app settings, and purchase state are primarily stored on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple StoreKit: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
